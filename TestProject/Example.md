## Health Systems Inc


## from patient to administration
- bio variables (blood pressure, oxigenation, glucose, etc) will turn into variables in the device connected to the patient.
- patient status (a set of bio variables that take into account the history of that variable in that patient) can be transmited or acquired by the flor management
- flor management has the responsability to check the patient general status, check for current and past status to identify effectiveness of treatment and manage the drugs added to the patient chart.
- the patient chart is controled by the attending doctor and the head nurse 
- the chart is both driven by doctor´s orders and feed by nurses who administer the medications
